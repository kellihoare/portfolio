About Me

My name is Kelli. I'm 19 years old, and I am studying at Otago Polytech. I first got into drawing when I was 10, when my sister, who was also an artist, moved to Australia.

When I started drawing, I drew a lot of cartoon characters, and I still do a lot of cartoon work now. When I started, I did pencil drawings; I have now started doing horror characters and floral pencil drawings. I then started working with watercolors, where I continued with cartoon work and found a love for painting and drawing frogs.

I have now started digital art, where I have begun exploring nature themes. For the future, I hope to build on my art style and learn more skills.

Art has been something that has been a part of me for so long, and it is something that I will always love doing. I can't wait to continue to watch my art grow and see where it takes me in the future.
